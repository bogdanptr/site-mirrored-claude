GPU racks designed for the latest AI workloads are pushing power densities well beyond what air cooling can handle efficiently.

## Direct-to-chip cooling

Liquid loops carry heat away from the chip far more efficiently than air, allowing higher densities per rack.

## Modular delivery

Like other Giga Box products, the hydro pod is designed to arrive largely pre-assembled, reducing on-site commissioning time.

## Integration with existing sites

The pod is designed to interface with standard chilled water loops, making it easier to integrate into existing facilities.
