Power capacity planning determines how much electrical infrastructure a site needs, both on day one and as it scales over time.

## Forecasting future load

Plans need to account not just for current IT load but for future expansion, which often means oversizing key infrastructure upfront.

## Redundancy requirements

Depending on the tier of reliability required, capacity plans may need to include N+1 or 2N redundancy across critical systems.

## Working with the utility

Capacity plans are only as good as the power available from the grid, so early conversations with the local utility are essential.
