Most data centers connect at distribution voltage, but the largest AI factory campuses sometimes need their own high-voltage substation.

## Why go high-voltage

Connecting closer to the transmission grid can unlock significantly more capacity than a standard distribution feed.

## Longer lead times

High-voltage equipment and utility coordination typically take longer, so this decision needs to be made early in planning.

## Is it worth it

For sites with very large, long-term power needs, the upfront investment in a high-voltage substation can pay off through greater capacity and reliability.
