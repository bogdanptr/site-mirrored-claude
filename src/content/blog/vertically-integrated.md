Vertical integration means controlling more steps of the build process in-house, from manufacturing equipment to managing site construction.

## Manufacturing in-house

Building transformers and switchgear internally reduces dependency on third-party lead times that are often the biggest schedule risk.

## Single point of accountability

When one team owns design, manufacturing, and installation, issues get resolved faster because there's no finger-pointing between vendors.

## Cost predictability

Controlling more of the supply chain also means fewer markups and more predictable pricing for customers.
