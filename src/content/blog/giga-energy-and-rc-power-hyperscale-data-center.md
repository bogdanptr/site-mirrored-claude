When a hyperscale operator needed to bring a new site online ahead of schedule, the project required tight coordination between manufacturing and field teams.

## The challenge

The site's original timeline left little room for delays in equipment delivery, which is often the biggest bottleneck on large builds.

## The approach

By manufacturing key electrical equipment in parallel with site civil work, the project avoided the usual sequential delays.

## The result

The site reached energization weeks ahead of the original schedule, demonstrating the value of integrated planning.
