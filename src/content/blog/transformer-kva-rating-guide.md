A transformer's kVA rating determines how much load it can safely carry, and getting it right matters for both cost and reliability.

## What kVA measures

kVA represents the total apparent power a transformer can deliver, combining both the real and reactive power components of a load.

## Calculating your load

Adding up the connected load and applying an appropriate diversity factor gives a realistic estimate of actual demand.

## Leaving room to grow

Sizing a transformer with some headroom above current load avoids costly replacements if demand increases sooner than expected.
