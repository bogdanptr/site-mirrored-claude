Lead times and pricing for power equipment can shift quickly, and teams planning AI factory or data center builds need a reliable way to track those changes.

## What the tool covers

The market data tool pulls together pricing and availability signals across transformers, switchgear, and substation equipment, giving project teams a clearer picture of what to expect when planning a build.

## Why it matters

Having a single place to check current market conditions helps teams budget more accurately and avoid surprises later in the procurement process.
