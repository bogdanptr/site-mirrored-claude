Most demand response platforms require users to become customers before they can access power market data. Giga Energy argues that this creates unnecessary friction, especially in markets like SPP and ERCOT where prices change constantly.

In fast-moving power markets, even an hour of delay can determine whether an operator captures a profitable curtailment window or misses it entirely.

Giga Energy has launched a free public market data dashboard at markets.gigaenergy.com to give operators and analysts easier access to real-time market signals.

## Why Giga built the market data dashboard

Giga Power Systems runs automated dispatch optimization for flexible load sites in SPP and ERCOT. To operate effectively, the team needed a continuous view of real-time market activity.

The internal dashboard was originally built to help the team monitor:

- Day-ahead and real-time LMPs
- Price separation
- Ancillary markets
- Generation mix
- Node-level market behavior
- Other operational market signals

As the tool became more useful internally, Giga decided to make it public.

The article argues that many operators and analysts face the same visibility problem. Official ISO portals are useful, but they are designed more for compliance than for fast operational interpretation. Commercial tools exist, but they are often priced for trading desks rather than site operators, engineers, or flexible load teams.

The public dashboard is the same signal layer Giga Power Systems uses internally. It is free, does not require a contract, and does not require a sales call.

## What the Giga Power Systems market data tool shows

The tool provides a clean view of SPP and ERCOT signals relevant to flexible load operations.

It includes:

- Day-ahead and real-time LMP data
- LMP component breakdowns, including MLC, MCC, and MEC
- Ancillary market data, including Spin, Reg Up, Reg Down, Supplemental, and Ramp
- Generation mix by fuel type
- Available generation capacity
- Load forecasts versus actuals
- Wind forecasts versus actuals
- Solar forecasts versus actuals
- Generation outages by fuel type
- A grid map covering approximately 1,600 plants and 2,000 nodes across SPP and ERCOT
- A custom Playground for multi-layer market analysis

The LMP component breakdown is especially useful because the final price only shows what cleared, while the components help explain what is happening on the grid.

For operators, this context can help explain why a specific node priced the way it did and can support better decisions in future operating windows.

## The Playground feature

The Playground allows users to compare multiple market signals directly inside the tool.

Instead of downloading data from several sources and building spreadsheets manually, users can layer signals such as:

- Real-time LMPs
- Ancillary clearing prices
- Generation mix
- Time-window-specific market behavior

This type of analysis previously required either a commercial market data subscription or significant manual work.

## How the tool connects to Giga Power Systems

The public market data tool can be used independently by operators who simply want better visibility into SPP and ERCOT prices.

For operators who want to go further, Giga Power Systems offers managed optimization services, including:

- Hour-by-hour dispatch optimization
- Enrollment in demand response programs
- Enrollment in ancillary markets
- Telemetry
- Compliance
- Fleet-level reporting

The same data that powers the public dashboard is also used by Giga's internal team for operational decisions.

## Why this matters for flexible load operators

Many flexible load operators participate in power markets without having a clear view of the signals driving those markets.

Giga's argument is that better access to power market data helps operators make better decisions, whether they are already enrolled in demand response programs or still evaluating whether market participation makes sense.

The dashboard is positioned as a free signal layer for understanding market behavior in SPP and ERCOT.

When operators are ready to turn that data into revenue, Giga Power Systems presents itself as a partner for dispatch optimization and demand response execution.
