The fastest path to ready-for-service (RFS) starts long before construction — it starts with the design choices made in the earliest planning stages.

## Modular over custom

Standardized, repeatable modules can be manufactured in parallel with site work, compressing the overall schedule.

## Early utility coordination

Engaging the local utility early on interconnection requirements avoids delays that are difficult to recover from later.

## Designing for phased energization

Breaking a site into smaller energization phases lets teams bring capacity online incrementally rather than waiting for the entire build to finish.
