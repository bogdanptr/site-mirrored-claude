The terms "AI factory" and "AI data center" are often used interchangeably, but the design priorities behind each can be quite different.

## Density and power

AI factories are built around extremely high power density per rack, driven by GPU clusters that consume far more energy than traditional compute.

## Cooling approach

Where traditional data centers rely heavily on air cooling, AI factories increasingly lean on liquid cooling to manage heat at scale.

## Speed to energization

AI factory projects are often judged on how quickly they can reach full power, which shapes everything from site selection to equipment procurement.
