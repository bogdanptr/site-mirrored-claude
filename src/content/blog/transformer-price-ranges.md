Transformer pricing varies widely depending on voltage class, kVA rating, and current market demand. Understanding the main drivers can help teams set realistic budgets early in a project.

## Key cost drivers

Voltage class, kVA capacity, copper and steel prices, and manufacturer lead times all play a role in determining transformer cost.

## Planning ahead

Because lead times can stretch out during periods of high demand, locking in equipment early is often as important as getting the price right.
