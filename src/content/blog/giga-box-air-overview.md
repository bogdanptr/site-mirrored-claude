Not every workload requires liquid cooling, and prefabricated air-cooled pods remain a fast, reliable option for many deployments.

## Built for speed

Because the pod arrives largely pre-wired and tested, on-site installation time is dramatically reduced compared to stick-built rooms.

## Flexible placement

Pods can be sited on a pad with minimal civil work, making them a good fit for brownfield sites or phased expansions.

## When it makes sense

For workloads that don't require the highest densities, an air-cooled pod can be the most cost-effective path to capacity.
