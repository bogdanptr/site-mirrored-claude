Finding a parcel of land is the easy part — finding one that's actually ready to support a large power-hungry build is much harder.

## Power availability

The single biggest factor is whether the site has access to enough power, or a credible path to get it, within an acceptable timeframe.

## Zoning and entitlements

Sites with industrial zoning already in place avoid months of entitlement work that can derail a project timeline.

## Site conditions

Flat, buildable land with good soil conditions and access to water and fiber rounds out a strong site profile.
